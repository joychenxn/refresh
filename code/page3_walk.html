Schenley Park

Schenley Park Loop Trail

Duration: 1 Hour

Schenley Park Loop Trail is a 2 mile loop trail located near CMU and is good for all skill levels. The trail offers a number of activity options and is accessible from May until September. Dogs are also able to use this trail.

Shadyside

Around Walnut Street

Duration: 30 minutes

The road from CMU to Walnut Street, the shopping area of Shadyside neighborhood, is quite enjoyable in spring, summer, and autumn. Start from the crossroad of Morewood and Fifth. Walk along Castleman Street and Pembroke Street till Aiken Street, you'll get well refreshed.

Campus

The Cut of CMU

Duration: 5-20 minutes

Give yourself no more than 20 minutes, and take a walk from Hunt Library across the cut to the Walking to the Sky sculpture. Stop thinking about homework and breath deeply. Everything will be fine!
