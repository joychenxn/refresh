Today's Recipe

Make cold-brew iced coffee in 5 minutes

1. Add water to coffee and stir
2. Cover it and leave it out on the counter overnight
3. A quick two-step filtering the next day(strain the grounds through a sieve, and use a coffee filter to pick up silt)
4. Dilute the brew one-to-one with water
5. You're done!
